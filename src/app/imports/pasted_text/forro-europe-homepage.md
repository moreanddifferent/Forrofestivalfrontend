CARD RULES
Each card must include:
- one strong photo
- festival name
- city, country
- dates
- one status chip only

Allowed status chips:
- Opens [date]
- Tickets available
- Tickets not announced
- Sold out

Do not add long descriptions inside cards.
Keep cards slightly compact, with strong image hierarchy and clean metadata.
Use real-looking destination-led imagery, not generic flyers.

IMAGE DIRECTION RULE
Prefer strong destination or atmosphere photography over weak poster visuals.
This website should feel like a travel guide for dancers, not only an events directory.

IMPORTANT
- do not invent fake festivals
- use the exact festivals above
- avoid duplicate cards across categories
- make the page feel believable as a real product
- keep the interface premium, airy, and easy to scan
- preserve emotional warmth and cultural specificity without clutter
